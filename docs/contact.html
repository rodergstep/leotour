About Lviv

At the beginning of the XXI century Lviv became the cultural and tourist capital of Ukraine. The unique atmosphere of the streets and squares, cafes and restaurants, parks and public garden, architectural monuments attract tourists from around the world. The uniqueness of the city is recognized by UNESCO.
